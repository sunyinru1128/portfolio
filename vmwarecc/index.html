How to handle complex ecosystem redesigns?

Redesigning a core landing page for millions of users with legacy systems is not just an interface update, it requires orchestrating a multi-disciplinary effort. The key is breaking down the holistic vision into actionable milestones, aligning priorities with product management, and collaborating deeply with engineering to ensure technical feasibility of interactions while adhering to strict accessibility guidelines. The 21% boost in CSAT is a testament to user-centric thinking.
